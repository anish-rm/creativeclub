About Us

Ravi Varma Creative Club

Introducing you to KEC's The Creative club. One place to showcase all your extraordinary and unique talents. A newly emerging club in KEC looking for young talents who get to participate in various competitions conducted by the club winning prizes and certificates. In the history of creativity, be prepared to witness a club which is not only a place to showcase your talent but also a paradise of artistry and imagination where the only limit is creativity. We are looking forward for your participation in our club where anybody can MAKE THEIR OWN WORLD
